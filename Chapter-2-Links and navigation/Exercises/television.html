Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a sci-fi horror series set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. When a young boy vanishes, a group of kids uncover a mysterious girl with supernatural abilities, a secretive government lab, and a terrifying alternate dimension known as the Upside Down.

Visit Official Website

Key Information

Genre: Science Fiction, Horror, Drama
Created by: The Duffer Brothers
First Released: July 15, 2016
Network: Netflix

Cast & Creative Team

  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Created by: Matt and Ross Duffer

Characters

Eleven: A girl with psychokinetic powers who escapes from a government lab.
Mike: The leader of the group of boys searching for their missing friend.
Hopper: The town's chief of police who uncovers sinister secrets.
Dustin & Lucas: Loyal friends with scientific curiosity and bravery.

Reviews

"Stranger Things is a masterclass in nostalgic storytelling mixed with horror and heart. The performances are compelling, and the visual style evokes the best of 80s cinema." – IGN

"A thrilling blend of adventure and science fiction with lovable characters and plenty of twists." – The Verge
