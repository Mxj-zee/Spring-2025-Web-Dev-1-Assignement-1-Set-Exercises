Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, comforting, and incredibly easy to make with just a few ingredients. Perfect for a quick and satisfying meal!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy. If using garlic, add it and sauté for 1 minute, then discard the garlic.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Remove the pan from heat and add the drained spaghetti. Quickly pour in the egg mixture, tossing everything to coat the pasta evenly.
  5. Add a splash of the reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce (add more if needed).
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan, black pepper, and parsley if desired.
